Product Care Guide

Automatic Timepieces

To maintain the precision of your automatic watch, we recommend wearing it daily or using a watch winder. Ensure the crown is always fully pushed in or screwed down before exposure to water.

Fine Jewelry

Avoid contact with perfumes, hairsprays, and household chemicals. Professional ultrasonic cleaning is recommended every 12 months.
