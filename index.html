Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence Spanning Three Centuries

Founded in Geneva in 1892, Bayshore+ began as a small atelier dedicated to creating timepieces of uncompromising quality. For over 130 years, our master craftsmen have preserved traditional techniques while embracing innovation.

Each piece that bears the Bayshore+ name undergoes rigorous testing and meticulous hand-finishing. From the initial sketch to the final polish, we invest countless hours ensuring every creation meets our exacting standards.
