Returns & Refunds

Return Window

We offer a 7-day return window from the date of delivery for all full-priced items. This period allows you to carefully inspect your timepiece or jewelry piece in person and ensure it meets your expectations. The return period begins at 12:00 AM local time on the delivery date confirmed by our courier tracking system.

For bespoke or customized pieces, including engraved items and made-to-order commissions, all sales are final unless the item arrives damaged or defective. Pre-owned and vintage pieces are also considered final sale due to their unique nature.

To initiate a return, please contact our concierge team before shipping any items back to us. Unauthorized returns will not be accepted.

Eligibility Conditions

To qualify for a full refund, returned items must meet the following conditions:

  • The item must be unworn and in pristine condition, showing no signs of use, alteration, or wear
  • All original packaging must be intact, including the presentation box, outer shipping box, and all protective materials
  • All accompanying documentation must be included: certificate of authenticity, warranty card, instruction manual, and any additional accessories
  • Security seals and tags must remain unbroken and attached to the item
  • The original receipt or proof of purchase must be provided

Items that show signs of wear, damage caused by handling, or missing components will be subject to a restocking fee of up to 20% of the purchase price, or may be returned to the customer at our discretion.

Damaged Product Handling

If your Bayshore+ piece arrives damaged or defective, we will arrange an immediate replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you. Please follow these steps:

  • Do not remove the item from its packaging if damage is visible on the exterior
  • Photograph the damaged packaging from multiple angles before opening
  • Document any damage to the item itself with detailed photographs
  • Contact our client care team within 24 hours of delivery with your order number and photographic evidence
  • Retain all original packaging materials for courier inspection

Our quality assurance team will review your claim within one business day. Approved damage claims will receive priority handling for replacement or refund processing.

Refund Timeline

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund within 5-10 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Day 1-2

Return received and logged into our authentication system

Day 3-5

Quality inspection and condition verification by our horologists and gemologists

Day 6-8

Refund approval and processing initiation

Day 9-10

Funds returned to original payment method; confirmation email sent

Please note that depending on your financial institution, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear on your statement. International refunds may experience additional delays due to currency conversion and cross-border banking processes.

Exchange Policy

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item for a different model or size, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for your preferred selection. This ensures the fastest processing time and secures your desired item, as our inventory of certain pieces is extremely limited.

Return Shipping

For standard returns within the 7-day window, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs and insurance. We strongly recommend using a tracked, insured courier service with signature confirmation and full value declaration.

Bayshore+ is not liable for items lost or damaged during return transit. For damaged product returns or warranty claims, we will provide a prepaid shipping label at our expense.

Need Assistance?

Our dedicated client care team is available to guide you through the return process and answer any questions regarding your purchase.
