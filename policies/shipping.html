Shipping Policy

Order Processing

All Bayshore+ timepieces and fine jewelry are hand-inspected and authenticated before shipment. Orders placed before 2:00 PM CET Monday through Friday are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

Due to the bespoke nature of certain pieces and the security protocols required for high-value items, some orders may require additional verification time. You will be notified via email if your order requires extended processing.

Each item is individually packaged in our signature presentation boxes with reinforced security seals and tamper-evident packaging.

Shipping Timeframes

Domestic Shipping (Switzerland)

  • Complimentary Express Delivery: 1-2 business days
  • White Glove Service (Available for purchases over CHF 50,000): Same-day delivery in select cities
  • All domestic shipments require signature confirmation and valid identification

International Shipping

  • European Union: 2-4 business days
  • United Kingdom & Norway: 3-5 business days
  • United States & Canada: 3-5 business days
  • Asia-Pacific: 4-7 business days
  • Middle East: 4-6 business days

International deliveries are handled exclusively through insured courier services with full tracking capabilities. All shipments are declared at full purchase value for insurance and customs purposes.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email containing your tracking number and estimated delivery date. You may track your shipment through the courier's website or by contacting our concierge team directly.

For security purposes, tracking information may be limited until the package reaches its destination country. Our client care team is available 24/7 to provide updates on your shipment's status.

International Customs & Duties

All shipments are delivered duties unpaid (DDU) unless otherwise specified. Recipients are responsible for all applicable customs duties, taxes, and import fees imposed by the destination country.

These charges are determined by local customs authorities and are not included in your purchase price or shipping cost. We recommend contacting your local customs office for specific duty rates before placing international orders.

Bayshore+ cannot declare items as gifts or reduce declared values to avoid customs duties, as this would violate international shipping regulations and void our insurance coverage.

Lost or Damaged Packages

While we take every precaution to ensure safe delivery, we understand that exceptional circumstances may occur. All Bayshore+ shipments are fully insured against loss or damage during transit.

Damage Upon Arrival

If your package arrives with visible damage to the exterior packaging, please refuse delivery and immediately contact our client care team. If damage is discovered after acceptance, notify us within 24 hours of delivery with photographic documentation.

Lost Shipments

If your tracking information shows delivery but you have not received your package, contact us immediately. We will initiate a trace investigation with the courier. If the package cannot be located within 10 business days, we will file an insurance claim and arrange a replacement or refund at our discretion.

Theft Prevention

For high-value orders exceeding CHF 100,000, we require delivery to a secure location and may arrange personal delivery by an Bayshore+ representative or authorized agent.

Special Circumstances

During peak holiday seasons, adverse weather conditions, or unforeseen global events, shipping times may be extended. We will communicate any delays promptly and offer alternative arrangements when possible.

For time-sensitive gifts or special occasions, we recommend placing orders at least two weeks in advance or contacting our concierge to arrange expedited handling.
