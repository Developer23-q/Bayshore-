Description

The Royal Oak Chronograph stands as a testament to bold design and horological mastery. First introduced in 1972, this iconic timepiece revolutionized luxury sports watches with its octagonal bezel and integrated bracelet.

The 41mm case houses the legendary Caliber 2385, a self-winding chronograph movement visible through the sapphire exhibition caseback. Each component is hand-finished by our master watchmakers in Geneva.

The Grande Tapisserie dial features three subsidiary dials for precise time measurement, while the date window at 4:30 maintains perfect symmetry.
